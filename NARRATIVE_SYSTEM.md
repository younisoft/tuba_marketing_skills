# NARRATIVE_SYSTEM.md

> **This is the highest-level creative document in the entire Tuba repository.** If you are a Creative Director joining this brand — five years from now, or five minutes from now — read this before `AI_KNOWLEDGE_PLATFORM.md`, before `ADVERTISING_IDENTITY_GUIDE.md`, before any campaign brief. Those documents will tell you *how to execute*. This one tells you how Tuba thinks, feels, sees, speaks, remembers, and tells stories — the layer beneath every execution, the one that should still be true long after any specific campaign built on it is forgotten.

---

## The One Thing to Know

Everything in this document, and in the fourteen-document [`narrative-system/`](narrative-system/) folder beneath it, exists to carry one idea forward: **[Everyone's Tuba Is Different](BIG_IDEA_PLATFORM.md)** — the discovery that belonging is not a shared fantasy a brand can sell everyone at once, but a radically personal thing, different for every single person, that this brand's own name (طوبى — blessedness, and the tree of paradise whose one root shades countless different homes) turns out to already carry.

This document does not re-argue that idea. `BIG_IDEA_PLATFORM.md` already made the case. This document is what happens *after* an idea is approved — the world it needs to live in for a decade.

---

## How Tuba Thinks

Tuba believes belonging is earned specifically, never granted generically — that every person is carrying a different history, a different fear, a different definition of "enough" into their search for a place, and that a brand which shows one dream and asks everyone to want it is selling conformity with better lighting, not belonging. This belief is stated in full in [`narrative-system/NARRATIVE_MANIFESTO.md`](narrative-system/NARRATIVE_MANIFESTO.md), and it is not a marketing position — it is closer to a philosophy Tuba would hold even if it sold nothing at all.

## How Tuba Feels

Belonging first, always — with Relief, Warmth, and a private, unperformed Pride close behind it. Twelve emotional territories, mapped precisely for when each should lead, support, or never appear at all, live in [`narrative-system/EMOTIONAL_SYSTEM.md`](narrative-system/EMOTIONAL_SYSTEM.md). The single governing emotional rule: no story here carries more than two emotions at real weight simultaneously, and none of them is ever manufactured through fear.

## How Tuba Sees

Human-first, eye-level, warm-lit, textured, specific — never the aspirational wide shot that makes architecture the hero and people small inside it. The full perceptual philosophy — composition, scale, light, shadow, material — lives in [`narrative-system/VISUAL_LANGUAGE.md`](narrative-system/VISUAL_LANGUAGE.md), extended into how the world moves ([`CINEMATIC_LANGUAGE.md`](narrative-system/CINEMATIC_LANGUAGE.md), [`MOTION_LANGUAGE.md`](narrative-system/MOTION_LANGUAGE.md)) and how it's photographed ([`PHOTOGRAPHY_LANGUAGE.md`](narrative-system/PHOTOGRAPHY_LANGUAGE.md)). The test behind all four: would this look like something a person paused to actually notice, or something built to be photographed?

## How Tuba Speaks

In ten relational modes — asking, listening, celebrating, comforting, guiding, thanking, congratulating, welcoming, responding to criticism, celebrating milestones — mapped in [`narrative-system/CONVERSATION_SYSTEM.md`](narrative-system/CONVERSATION_SYSTEM.md) and shown in real, specific situations in [`narrative-system/VOICE_IN_ACTION.md`](narrative-system/VOICE_IN_ACTION.md). The test behind every mode: could this exact message have been sent to anyone, about anything? If yes, it hasn't listened yet.

## How Tuba Remembers

Through symbols that recur without ever being explained — a door, a threshold, shade, a specific chair, a height chart in pencil — catalogued with meaning, emotion, and usage discipline in [`narrative-system/SYMBOL_LIBRARY.md`](narrative-system/SYMBOL_LIBRARY.md). Through cultural truths held with real specificity and zero stereotype in [`narrative-system/CULTURAL_TRUTHS.md`](narrative-system/CULTURAL_TRUTHS.md). Through a hundred small, unowned observations about people in general in [`narrative-system/HUMAN_TRUTHS.md`](narrative-system/HUMAN_TRUTHS.md) — the emotional bedrock every future story quietly stands on without ever quoting directly.

## How Tuba Tells Stories

Through eight universal beats — Beginning, Conflict, Search, Choice, Transformation, Arrival, Reflection, Hope — mapped in [`narrative-system/STORY_ARCHITECTURE.md`](narrative-system/STORY_ARCHITECTURE.md), populated by nine recurring human archetypes in [`narrative-system/CHARACTER_ARCHETYPES.md`](narrative-system/CHARACTER_ARCHETYPES.md), drawing from a catalog of real, small [`LIFE_MOMENTS.md`](narrative-system/LIFE_MOMENTS.md), organized into eight long-term [`NARRATIVE_PILLARS.md`](narrative-system/NARRATIVE_PILLARS.md), and stocked with 498 individually-written starting points in [`narrative-system/STORY_LIBRARY.md`](narrative-system/STORY_LIBRARY.md) — enough raw material that no future creative team should ever need to reinvent this brand from nothing.

## The World This All Lives In

[`narrative-system/WORLD_BUILDING.md`](narrative-system/WORLD_BUILDING.md) draws the boundaries: specific people, never types; unfinished lives, never static achievement; real weather and real light; quiet competence over showy rescue; Saudi Arabia specifically, confidently, in all its regional range — never an abstracted "Middle East." And just as importantly, what never belongs here: aspirational strangers, empty hero-shot houses, glass towers as shorthand for success, manufactured urgency, brand-as-hero, literal religious iconography, status display, or condescension toward beginners.

**The one-sentence test that governs everything:** if a story could be set in any real estate brand's universe without changing a single detail, it does not belong in this one.

## Where This Goes Next

[`narrative-system/CAMPAIGN_UNIVERSE.md`](narrative-system/CAMPAIGN_UNIVERSE.md) shows this single idea expressing itself differently across thirteen genuinely different contexts — Ramadan, Eid, National Day, investment, luxury, family, youth, developers, brokers, CSR, education, recruitment, partnerships — never becoming a different idea, only a different lens on the same one. [`narrative-system/FUTURE_EXPANSION.md`](narrative-system/FUTURE_EXPANSION.md) imagines further still: films, podcasts, books, physical spaces, a decade out, none of it losing what makes this recognizably Tuba's.

None of this is a campaign. None of it is a headline. [`narrative-system/README.md`](narrative-system/README.md) states the rule plainly: this folder produces no assets — the next phase produces executions from it, using the entire execution layer this narrative universe now sits above: [`advertising-system/`](advertising-system/), [`brand-intelligence/`](brand-intelligence/), [`context-packs/`](context-packs/), and every tool the [`AI_KNOWLEDGE_PLATFORM.md`](AI_KNOWLEDGE_PLATFORM.md) already routes a future creative team through.

## The Test for Everything, Forever

Before anything ships under this brand — a headline, a film, a building, a decade-old campaign or one written tomorrow — one question, asked plainly:

> **Does this feel like it could only have come from Tuba?**

If the answer is yes, it belongs here. If the answer is no, it belongs to the category this entire system was built to leave behind.
