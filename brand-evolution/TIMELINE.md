## 2026-07-14 — Platform Founding & Full Build

The complete platform was built in a single, phased engagement, in this order:

### Phase 1-3 — Discovery (`knowledge-base/`)
Deep research into the existing Tuba brand: the official 8-page logo guideline PDF, all logo/icon SVG source files, the live tuba.com.sa platform, and extensive competitor research (Aqar, Bayut, Wasalt, Deal/Daleel, Sakani, REGA, NHC). Produced [project-analysis.md](../knowledge-base/project-analysis.md), [current-brand-analysis.md](../knowledge-base/current-brand-analysis.md), [competitor-analysis.md](../knowledge-base/competitor-analysis.md).

### Phase 4-12 — Advertising Identity System (`advertising-system/`, strategy layer)
Built the strategic and execution core: brand DNA, visual system, iconography, copywriting system (adapting a content methodology from an internal IKEA content-strategy reference document), content system, social media playbook, campaign playbook, design components.

### Phase 13 — Advertising Operating System (`advertising-system/`, execution layer)
Added the process layer that makes the strategy actually usable day to day: checklists, Mermaid-diagrammed workflows, brief templates, scored review frameworks, AI prompt library, prompt-engineering patterns, asset library governance, visual decision trees, messaging framework, psychological triggers with guardrails, and the master advertising playbook. Also produced the root [CLAUDE_SYSTEM_PROMPT.md](../CLAUDE_SYSTEM_PROMPT.md).

### Phase 14 — Brand Intelligence & Brand Memory
Built two complementary layers: `brand-intelligence/` (25 evergreen documents — market psychology, language layers, five persona families, journey mapping, creative libraries, ideation systems, strategic frameworks) and `brand-memory/` (17 living-log documents — fully templated and governed, but deliberately left unpopulated pending real campaign activity, per this platform's strict no-fabrication standard).

### Phase 15 — AI Knowledge Graph, Retrieval System & Quality Engine
Transformed the documentation set into an AI-native retrieval platform: `knowledge-graph/` (entity/relationship/taxonomy/ontology modeling plus AI-first navigation), `context-packs/` (11 compressed, ≤300-line domain summaries), `routing/` (100+ task-to-document mappings plus visual decision trees), `metadata/` (the frontmatter/tagging/naming/lifecycle standards), `evaluation/` (scorecards and quality gates for brand/copy/design/campaign/prompt output), and this `brand-evolution/` folder itself. Culminated in the root [AI_KNOWLEDGE_PLATFORM.md](../AI_KNOWLEDGE_PLATFORM.md) architecture document.
