# Design Scorecard

> **Part of:** [AI_KNOWLEDGE_PLATFORM.md](../AI_KNOWLEDGE_PLATFORM.md)
> **Purpose:** the fast, 10-dimension version of design evaluation — for quick iterative checks during production. The full, 15-dimension formal gate-review (including CTA Visibility, Brand Consistency, Luxury/Professional/Trust Perception, Emotional Clarity) already exists at [../advertising-system/design-review-checklist.md](../advertising-system/design-review-checklist.md); **use that for formal Approval-gate review.** This scorecard adds two dimensions not broken out there (Icons, Motion) and is meant for faster, mid-production self-checks.
> **Owner:** Design lead
> **Review frequency:** semi-annually, synced with design-review-checklist.md's own review cycle

---

## Scoring Dimensions (1-10 each)

### 1. Layout
One clear focal point, deliberate composition — maps to design-review-checklist.md §1 (Hierarchy).

### 2. Spacing
8px scale followed consistently, no cramped or uneven gaps — maps to design-review-checklist.md §3.

### 3. Typography
Correct typefaces, max 3 sizes, correct bilingual parity — maps to design-review-checklist.md §6.

### 4. Contrast
Enough visual difference for hierarchy to read at a glance; WCAG-compliant text contrast — maps to design-review-checklist.md §2, §10.

### 5. Photography
Warm, real, straightened, negative space for text — maps to design-review-checklist.md §7.

### 6. Icons (new dimension)
Purple-only, one treatment (filled/outline) held consistently, proportional scaling. Reference: [iconography.md](../advertising-system/iconography.md).

### 7. Color
Correct 60/30/10 ratio, Coral used ≤2x, no forbidden pairings — maps to design-review-checklist.md §11.

### 8. Balance
Composition feels weighted correctly, not lopsided unless intentional — maps to design-review-checklist.md §4.

### 9. Motion (new dimension, video/animated assets only)
Soft ease-in-out, no spin/bounce/distort on the mark, gradient never flashes. Reference: [visual-system.md §8](../advertising-system/visual-system.md). N/A for static assets.

### 10. Accessibility
Contrast ratios, tap target size, no color-only meaning — maps to design-review-checklist.md §10.

## Relationship to the Formal Review

This scorecard is a **lighter, faster tool** — pass here does not substitute for [design-review-checklist.md](../advertising-system/design-review-checklist.md)'s full 15-dimension scoring at the formal Approval gate (campaign-workflows.md §6). Use this scorecard during production/iteration; use the full checklist before an asset moves to Approval.

---

## Best Practices
- Use this scorecard for quick self-checks between drafts; switch to the full design-review-checklist.md before formal submission
- Score Icons and Motion honestly even though they're "new" dimensions — they catch two of the most common off-brand mistakes (mixed icon treatments, illegal logo animation)

## Common Mistakes
- Treating this lighter scorecard as sufficient for final Approval — it isn't; see [../advertising-system/design-review-checklist.md](../advertising-system/design-review-checklist.md) for the formal gate
- Scoring Motion on a static asset — mark N/A
