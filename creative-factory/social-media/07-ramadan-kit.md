## Hooks

1. The prayer corner. The table. The chair by the window.
2. This isn't a sale. It's a month about what actually matters.
3. Every financing term, stated plainly, in the same place as the property.
4. However you're spending Ramadan, we're not going to rush you.
5. The room that isn't in any photo is the one you'll remember.
