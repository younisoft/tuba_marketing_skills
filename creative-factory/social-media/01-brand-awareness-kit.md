## Hooks (First-3-seconds / First-sentence, per content-hooks.md §1–2)

1. A paper price tag, still looped through a brass key.
2. Everyone says they want to help you find your dream home.
3. This door isn't the grand one. That's exactly why it's theirs.
4. What does "enough" actually look like to you?
5. A pencil mark on a wall, at exactly a child's height.

## CTAs (Awareness category)

1. See how it works — شوف كيف تسير الأمور
2. Find your Tuba — ابحث عن طوبتك
