## Hooks

1. A specific street in Diriyah. A specific shade of sky over the Corniche.
2. What does National Day actually look like where you're from?
3. Every flag looks the same. Every reason for flying it doesn't.
4. This year, we're not posting one picture. We're collecting thousands.
5. Your Saudi Arabia isn't a stock photo. Show us the real one.

## CTAs

1. Share yours — شاركنا وطنك *(participation CTA — deliberately not a transaction CTA; see campaign brief's Brand Review note on why "Request your property" was removed)*
