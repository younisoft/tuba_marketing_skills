## Hooks

1. The scariest part of buying your first home isn't the price.
2. "How much do I actually need saved?" — a real question, answered plainly.
3. Nobody tells you it's okay to not know the process yet.
4. What if something's wrong with the property that I can't see?
5. Every question you're afraid to ask is one we're used to answering.

## CTAs

1. Understand the process — افهم الخطوات *(top-of-funnel, educational content)*
2. Request your property — اطلب عقارك *(bottom-of-funnel, once ready — never on the same asset as #1)*
