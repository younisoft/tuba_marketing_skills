# Motion Treatments

> **Purpose:** the Motion Director's cut across all 8 campaigns — every campaign's Motion Direction section states its own governing instinct; this document adds the format-level specificity (Reel/Story/TikTok/video pacing) that would have bloated each campaign file if repeated eight times.
> **Governs every row:** [`../../advertising-system/visual-system.md`](../../advertising-system/visual-system.md) §8 (technical easing/transition specs — never violated below) and [`../../narrative-system/MOTION_LANGUAGE.md`](../../narrative-system/MOTION_LANGUAGE.md) (the philosophy those specs protect). The single test applied to every row: *if this moved at half the speed, would it still work?*

---

| Campaign | Governing motion instinct | Reel/Story pacing | What this campaign's motion never does |
|---|---|---|---|
| 01 Brand Awareness | Growth-based transitions, door-shaped match cuts | Full film: 60–90s, unhurried; TikTok cutdown: vignette 1 only, still no speed-ramp | Never speed-ramps toward the reveal; final beat holds 2s longer than any prior shot |
| 02 First Home Buyers | Calm, procedural — motion serves clarity, not emotion | Carousel-led (static swipe pacing); explainer Reels move step by step, one idea per beat | Never rushes the Reassure-stage content, regardless of format |
| 03 Property Investors | Data reveals as honest count-up/bar-fill, soft ease-out | Chart animations only; no photography-led motion | Never a flashy chart-reveal transition — the animation reveals a true number, doesn't perform excitement about it |
| 04 Real Estate Brokers | Understated — clarity over craft | Minimal motion use overall; a UI walkthrough only, if produced | No cinematic dissolve treatment — this register earns trust through content, not visual poetry |
| 05 Developers | Structured, measured — a clear walk from context to vision to ask | Partnership-announcement film only, no social-native cutdowns | Never uses the match-cut poetry of Campaign 01 — clarity is this campaign's craft |
| 06 Saudi National Day | Restrained, explicitly no countdown/urgency graphics | Rolling compilation Reels, re-cut monthly as submissions grow | Never a countdown timer, even during the run-up to September 23 — the single hardest-to-resist violation this campaign guards against |
| 07 Ramadan | The most restrained pacing in the entire deliverable — silence as a tool | Evening-lit, single-gesture Reels (the coffee pour); TikTok deprioritized as a poor pacing fit | Never fills every second with sound or cutting — Ramadan content is allowed to hold a quiet moment |
| 08 Corporate Brand | Minimal, plain — trust through plainness, not craft | No Reel/Story/TikTok production at all (channel-excluded, see campaign brief) | Never uses cinematic device to compensate for institutional register — plainness is correct here, not a limitation |
