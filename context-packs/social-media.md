# Context Pack — Social Media

> **Purpose:** the minimum knowledge for producing platform-native social content. **Full source:** [social-media-playbook.md](../advertising-system/social-media-playbook.md)

## Platform Priority (evidenced, not assumed)

| Tier | Platforms | Why |
|---|---|---|
| 1 — Primary | Snapchat, TikTok, Instagram | Where the Saudi RE audience actually engages — Aqar's largest channel is Snapchat (262K); Bayut's best-documented paid result is a Snapchat AR Lens |
| 2 — Strategic | LinkedIn, YouTube | B2B credibility + a confirmed competitive white space (near-zero competitor investment in YouTube) |
| 3 — Maintenance | X, Facebook, Pinterest, Threads | Announcement/service/archival — don't over-invest production here |

## Per-Platform Quick Specs

| Platform | Frequency | Hook rule | CTA style |
|---|---|---|---|
| Instagram | 5-6 posts/Reels weekly, daily Stories | Hook in first 3s of video or first caption line | Link-in-bio / Story sticker |
| TikTok | 4-5 videos/week | Sub-2s hook, text overlay + spoken simultaneously | On-screen text, singular |
| Snapchat | Daily Stories | Visual-first, image before text | Swipe-up / lens interaction |
| LinkedIn | 2-3 posts/week | Hook line, not a company-announcement lede | "Learn more" / "Partner with us" |
| X | Daily | Short, single-idea | Link to landing page |
| Facebook | 3-4 posts/week (re-cut, not cross-posted) | — | Standard |
| YouTube | 1-2 videos/week building to a series | First 5s states viewer benefit directly | Subscribe + description link |
| Pinterest | Low-effort, batch-monthly | — | Long-tail discovery, not real-time |

## Content Ratio (Instagram, representative)

40% Property Showcase · 30% Place & Lifestyle · 20% Guidance & Education · 10% Community

## Hard Rules

1. **Never cross-post identically** — every platform gets its own pacing/aspect-ratio cut
2. **Arabic-first, always** — primary account is Arabic; English is a deliberate secondary layer, not a mirror
3. **Same voice, different register** — tone shifts by platform (formal LinkedIn vs. casual TikTok), values never do
4. **FAL/trust signaling present but unobtrusive everywhere**
5. **Same-day response discipline** on X/Instagram DMs and comments — a "guide" brand cannot leave questions unanswered

## Format Safe Zones (Stories/Reels, 9:16)

Top 250px and bottom 350px are platform-UI dead zones — no essential text/logo there.

## Hashtag Discipline

3-5 targeted (city/district/property-type) + 1-2 brand tags. Never generic hashtag stuffing. TikTok: mix niche + broad discovery tags (#اكسبلور pattern).
