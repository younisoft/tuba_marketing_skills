## Opening Hook by Format

| Format | Rule | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Reel/short-form | Visual pattern-interrupt + text-overlay fragment, first 3 seconds | Attention decided before conscious evaluation |
| TikTok | Sub-2s hook, on-screen text + spoken simultaneously | Competing against an infinite fast-moving feed |
| Story | Immediate single-message clarity, no build-up | Consumed in rapid tap-through rhythm |
| YouTube (long-form) | State the viewer benefit directly within 5 seconds | Requires a larger attention commitment than short-form |

Full psychology behind each: [content-hooks.md](../brand-intelligence/content-hooks.md).

## Script Structure Template

```
[Timestamp] — [On-screen text] — [Spoken/voiceover line] — [Visual direction]
```
0-2s: Hook · 2-20s (short-form) or 5s-body (long-form): core content, one idea · Final 2-5s: CTA (spoken + on-screen text)

## Thumbnail Rules (video-system.md §9.4)

- Bold 3-5 word text overlay, high-contrast scrim
- Face or property hero shot, rule-of-thirds framing
- Small mark watermark, bottom corner, **consistent across all thumbnails** for channel recognizability

## Motion Principles

- Soft ease-in-out only — never linear or bouncy/elastic (contradicts calm, trustworthy tone)
- Logo may scale/fade in gently — never spin, bounce, or distort
- Transitions: cross-dissolve or soft slide, 200-400ms
- Gradient background may animate slowly (8-12s loop) — never flash/strobe
- Icon reveals: line-draw-on for outline icons, 400-600ms

## Platform-Specific Notes

- **TikTok/Reels:** raw-cut, native-feeling pacing — never a resized version of a different platform's edit
- **YouTube:** the confirmed category white-space channel (near-zero competitor investment) — build toward a recognizable weekly series (e.g., "حي الأسبوع" neighborhood-of-the-week)
- **Snapchat:** platform-native AR/interactive formats outperform repurposed video ads — budget for a custom Lens on major seasonal campaigns

## CTA in Video

Verbal + on-screen simultaneously, always singular. Example: "Comment your city and we'll find you three."

## Voice/Register in Video

Casual Saudi dialect permitted specifically in TikTok/Snapchat short-form (copywriting.md §7.1) — still warm and grounded, never salesy. Long-form YouTube uses a more measured, educational register.
