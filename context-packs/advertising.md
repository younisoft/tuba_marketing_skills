## Typography

| Role | Typeface |
|---|---|
| Arabic display/headline | Almarai (Bold/ExtraBold) |
| Arabic body | Almarai (Regular/Light) or IBM Plex Sans Arabic |
| Latin display/headline | Plus Jakarta Sans (Bold/ExtraBold) |
| Latin body | Plus Jakarta Sans (Regular/Medium) |

Never more than 3 type sizes per asset. Arabic runs 1-2px larger than Latin at equivalent weight. Western numerals for prices/specs.

## Spacing & Shape

- 8px spacing scale: 4·8·16·24·32·48·64·96px
- Border radius: buttons 12px/pill, cards 16-20px, badges full pill — **never sharp 0px corners** on brand-owned containers
- Shadows: purple-tinted only (`rgba(42,12,114,...)`), never neutral grey/black

## Icons

- Two families: real estate (house/villa/Saudi-house) + desert/lifestyle (sun/waves/palm/mountains)
- Each has filled + outline variants, stroke ≈1.72px, soft miter joints
- **Purple only** — Coral never fills an icon
- Pick ONE treatment (filled or outline) per composition, never mixed
- Full spec + gap roadmap: [iconography.md](../advertising-system/iconography.md)

## Photography Direction

- Real Saudi homes/people, never generic Western stock or anonymous skylines
- Warm, golden-hour-biased lighting; straightened verticals; negative space for text
- People shown in real moments, not posed handshake clichés
- Reserve glossy/editorial treatment for explicit luxury campaigns only

## Content Pillars (content-system.md §1)

| Pillar | Share of mix |
|---|---|
| Guidance & Education | 25% |
| Property Showcase | 25% |
| Place & Lifestyle | 20% |
| Market Insight & Investment | 15% |
| Community & Customer Success | 10% |
| Broker & Developer (B2B) | 5% (higher on LinkedIn) |

Trust & Compliance is woven through all pillars, not a separate bucket.

## Layout Templates (visual-system.md §9)

| Format | Key rule |
|---|---|
| Social feed (1:1/4:5) | Mark-only logo, one headline (≤8 words), one CTA |
| Carousel | Slide 1 = hook only; consistent header treatment across slides |
| Story/Reel (9:16) | Top 250px / bottom 350px = safe-zone dead space (platform UI) |
| Display/banner | Below 300×250: mark + 3-4 word headline + CTA only |
| Performance ad | Offer/price/location visible without interaction |
| OOH | One headline (3-5 words), test the "3 seconds from a moving car" rule |

## Component Basics (design-components.md)

- **Property card:** price in bold Coral/Purple numerals, FAL badge bottom corner
- **Primary CTA button:** Coral fill, white text, pill radius — **never a Purple-filled primary CTA** (most common mistake)
- **Trust badge:** pill shape, Purple 8 background, small verified-checkmark

## Color Hierarchy (the one rule that matters most)

```
60% Canvas/neutral · 30% Purple · 10% Coral
```
Coral is the exception color, not the wallpaper — if it exceeds ~15% of visual weight, it stops working.
