# Context Pack — SEO

> **Purpose:** the minimum knowledge for search-optimizing Tuba content without breaking brand voice. **Full sources:** [content-review-checklist.md §12](../advertising-system/content-review-checklist.md) · [real-estate-terminology.md](../brand-intelligence/real-estate-terminology.md) · [content-system.md](../advertising-system/content-system.md)

---

## Core Principle

SEO discipline and brand voice are not in tension — clarity and specificity (already core copywriting rules, [copywriting.md](../advertising-system/copywriting.md)) are exactly what search engines and readers both reward. **Never keyword-stuff** — it violates both SEO best practice and the brand's plain, grounded voice.

## Structural Rules

- Primary keyword/topic appears naturally in the headline and first paragraph
- Headers (H2/H3) structure long-form content logically, matching the type hierarchy in [visual-system.md §2.3](../advertising-system/visual-system.md)
- Meta description = a Hook-formula sentence ([headline-library.md](../brand-intelligence/headline-library.md)), not a generic summary
- Alt text on every image: descriptive and genuinely useful for accessibility, never keyword-stuffed

## Terminology Discipline

Use the canonical terms from [real-estate-terminology.md](../brand-intelligence/real-estate-terminology.md) consistently — search relevance depends partly on consistent, correct terminology (FAL, REGA, Ejar, price-per-square-meter, etc.), not synonym-cycling for variety.

## Content That Naturally Performs for SEO

Per content-system.md's Authority/Evergreen funnel roles:
- Educational/Guidance content (how financing works, FAL explainers) — long-tail, high search intent
- Market Insight reports ("دليل طوبة") — data-driven, citation-worthy, naturally link-earning
- Neighborhood/district content — matches the district-level (not just city-level) search behavior confirmed in [market-psychology.md §15](../brand-intelligence/market-psychology.md)

## Bilingual SEO Note

Arabic content is the primary search surface for the core Saudi audience — never treat the English version as the "real" content with Arabic as an afterthought translation (this is both an SEO and a brand-voice violation, [copywriting.md §7](../advertising-system/copywriting.md)).

## What NOT to Do

- Don't sacrifice the Guidance Framework's Hook stage for a keyword-first opening line — a search-optimized headline can still use an approved formula ([headline-library.md](../brand-intelligence/headline-library.md))
- Don't fabricate statistics or claims to fill out word count — every claim must be real and sourced (copywriting.md §5.7)
- Don't over-optimize at the expense of the one-CTA rule — SEO content still ends with a single clear action
