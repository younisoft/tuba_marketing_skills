## What Tuba Is

A Saudi digital real estate marketplace — buy, rent, off-plan projects, agent network, request-first property matching. Built around a FAL/REGA-licensed compliance backbone.

## Brand Promise

> **"You will never have to figure this out alone."**

Tuba doesn't advertise listings — it advertises relief. Every asset should make the viewer feel less alone in a hard decision.

## Brand Archetype

**Primary:** The Sage (Guide). **Secondary:** The Caregiver. A wise, warm friend who knows the Saudi real estate system inside and out — never The Ruler (biggest/best claims), never The Hero (Tuba isn't the protagonist — the customer is).

## The Logo & Mark

- Monogram: globe (circle) + Arabic ه / English "a" flipped upside-down + hut (rounded roof) — reads as a home silhouette
- Both Arabic and English wordmarks are circle-constructed — a genuine bilingual type system, not a translation
- Mark may stand alone; logotype never appears without the mark available in the same lockup family
- **Never:** move/rotate/recolor-per-element/distort the mark, add shadows, or place the two-tone color logo on a solid/gradient background (white/black only there)

## Color System

| Color | Hex | Role |
|---|---|---|
| Tuba Purple | `#2A0C72` | Dominant/authority — ~30% of any layout |
| Tuba Coral | `#F95A60` | Accent/warmth — ≤10%, used ≤2x per composition |
| Canvas/neutral | `#F7F5FC` / `#FFFFFF` | ~60% — the ground everything sits on |

**Never** introduce a third brand color. Icons are Purple-only — Coral never fills an icon (it's reserved for the mark, CTAs, and key accents).

## Why This Matters Competitively

Every credibly-researched category leader (Aqar, Bayut, even the NHC rebrand) sits on the **green** spectrum and leads with either scale claims or procedural trust-badging, not warmth. Purple/coral + an emotionally-guided voice is Tuba's most efficient, hardest-to-copy differentiator. Full evidence: [competitor-analysis.md §2, §11](../knowledge-base/competitor-analysis.md).

## Five Voice Adjectives

**Warm · Clear · Grounded · Guiding · Respectful** — never corporate, salesy, cold, or condescending.

## Brand Personality Dial

| Dimension | Setting |
|---|---|
| Luxury level | Accessible-premium by default; full luxury register only for explicit luxury campaigns |
| Professional level | High — licensed, regulated, high-stakes category |
| Confidence | Quiet, evidence-backed — never loud/superlative |
| Authority | Earned through transparency/data, never claimed through scale numbers |
| Innovation | Practical, quietly delivered — never a showy tech flex |

## Positioning Statement

> For Saudi property buyers, renters, sellers, agents, and developers who find real estate transactions confusing and impersonal, Tuba is the platform that guides you through the process with warmth, clarity, and verified trust — unlike competitors who lead with inventory scale or procedural trust-badges alone.

Full positioning map and pillars: [brand-positioning-framework.md](../brand-intelligence/brand-positioning-framework.md).

## The Four Trust Pillars

1. **Regulatory transparency** — FAL/REGA licensing shown as a design-integrated badge, never buried
2. **Process transparency** — fees, steps, timelines stated plainly
3. **Verification with enforcement** — a badge is only as good as what's actually behind it (see the Bayut cautionary case, [competitor-analysis.md §4](../knowledge-base/competitor-analysis.md))
4. **Human backup** — technology plus real support, never a pure self-serve black box

## Ten Non-Negotiables (the fast checklist)

1. Colors: Purple + Coral only, 60/30/10 ratio
2. Mark construction never redrawn/distorted/recolored
3. Icons Purple-only
4. One CTA per asset, always
5. FAL/license badge on every commerce-adjacent asset
6. Arabic is never a translation of English creative
7. No fear-based urgency, unverifiable superlatives, fabricated proof, or disparaging competitors
8. Customer is always the hero, Tuba is the guide
9. Every warmth claim backed by a real, checkable trust signal
10. Seasonal timing fixed to the real cultural calendar

## What Tuba Is NOT

- Not the biggest-inventory claim (that's Aqar's frame — don't compete there, [competitor-differentiation.md](../brand-intelligence/competitor-differentiation.md))
- Not a badge-first trust brand (that's Bayut's frame, imperfectly enforced)
- Not an imported/localized brand voice — built Saudi-first from the founding brief
- Not exclusive/status-first by default — that register is reserved for explicit luxury campaigns only
