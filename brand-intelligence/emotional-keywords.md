## 1. Relief (primary brand emotion)

**English:** finally, at last, no more guessing, taken care of, sorted, one less thing to worry about, straightforward, clear path, in good hands
**Arabic:** أخيراً، ارتحت، خلاص، بلا تعقيد، اطمأن قلبك، سهلناها عليك

**Best used at:** Reassure and Decide stages (copywriting.md §1)

## 2. Belonging

**English:** your place, home ground, where you belong, made for your life, a place that's yours, grow into
**Arabic:** مكانك، بيتك، تنتمي له، يناسب حياتك، يكبر معك

**Best used at:** Hook and Guide stages, Place & Lifestyle content (content-system.md pillar 3)

## 3. Confidence

**English:** clear, know exactly, no surprises, the real picture, backed by data, see it clearly
**Arabic:** واضح، تعرف بالضبط، بدون مفاجآت، الصورة الحقيقية، بيانات موثوقة

**Best used at:** Guide and Solve stages, Investment/Market Insight content (content-system.md pillar 2)

## 4. Warmth

**English:** together, alongside you, we've got you, welcome, gathered, close
**Arabic:** معاً، بجانبك، احنا موجودين، أهلاً، تجمعكم، قريب

**Best used at:** Guide stage, family/community content (content-system.md pillar 4)

## 5. Quiet Pride

**English:** yours, earned, worth it, proud of this, a decision you'll stand behind
**Arabic:** لك، تستحقه، يستاهل، فخور فيه، قرار تفتخر فيه

**Best used at:** Decide stage, ownership-moment and National Day content (campaign-playbook.md §1.1)

## 6. Words That Trigger the Wrong Emotion (avoid)

| Word/phrase | Wrong emotion it triggers | Use instead |
|---|---|---|
| "Don't miss out" | Anxiety/pressure, not relief | "Here when you're ready" |
| "Limited time" (without real deadline) | Manufactured urgency | State the real deadline, or omit |
| "The best" / "#1" | Skepticism (unverifiable claim) | A specific, checkable fact |
| "Luxurious" | Generic aspiration, not specific luxury (copywriting.md §5.3) | One concrete distinguishing detail |
| "Dear valued customer" | Distance, not warmth | Direct address ("you"/"أنت") |

## 7. Emotional Word Selection by Persona

| Persona | Lead emotion | Sample words |
|---|---|---|
| First-time buyer | Relief + Confidence | "step by step," "no surprises," "خطوة بخطوة" |
| Family buyer | Belonging + Warmth | "grow into," "together," "معاً" |
| Investor | Confidence | "clear," "backed by data," "واضح" |
| Luxury buyer | Quiet pride (restrained) | "yours," "considered," "لك" — never stacked with superlatives |
| Broker/Developer | Confidence + partnership warmth | "alongside you," "شريكك" |

## 8. Emotional Intensity Scale

Not every piece of copy should hit maximum emotional intensity — calibrate by funnel role (content-system.md §3):

| Funnel role | Emotional intensity |
|---|---|
| Awareness/Brand | High — full emotional vocabulary available |
| Lead generation | Medium — emotion opens, but specifics (price, location) carry the middle |
| Performance/conversion | Low-medium — clarity over feeling, per copywriting.md §6 |
| Retention | Medium — warmth without renewed urgency |
| B2B | Low-medium — professional warmth (copywriting.md §5.2), not consumer-intensity emotion |

## Best Practices
- Pick words from the emotion that matches the *stage* of the Guidance Framework being written, not just words that sound nice in isolation
- Cross-check every emotional word choice against copywriting.md §8.3's forbidden list before finalizing

## Common Mistakes
- Stacking multiple emotion-category words in one sentence (e.g., relief + pride + belonging all at once) — dilutes rather than intensifies, per copywriting.md §2.2's one-idea-per-sentence rule
- Using an emotion word without the concrete detail that would make a reader actually feel it (see prompt-patterns.md §5)
