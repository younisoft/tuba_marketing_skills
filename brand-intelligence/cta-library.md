## Selection Logic

1. Identify the funnel role of the asset (content-system.md §3)
2. Identify the persona (messaging-framework.md, or the deeper persona files in this folder)
3. Match to the CTA category above that fits both
4. Never combine two categories in one asset — one CTA, one category, always

## Best Practices
- CTA copy should describe the reader's literal next action in their own words (prompt-patterns.md §6), not a marketing abstraction
- Luxury and Consultation-category CTAs should always be softer/relationship-first than Lead Generation-category CTAs — matching intensity to persona sensitivity (buyer-personas.md §2, §6)

## Common Mistakes
- Using a high-commitment CTA ("Request your property") at a low-intent, Stage 1-2 touchpoint (customer-journey.md §2) — mismatched intensity kills conversion
- Defaulting to "Contact us today" instead of a specific category CTA — explicitly forbidden, copywriting.md §8.3
