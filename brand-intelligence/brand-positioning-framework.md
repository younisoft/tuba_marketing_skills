> **Layer:** Brand Intelligence (static)
> **Purpose:** the formal positioning-statement construction and positioning map — where [advertising-dna.md](../advertising-system/advertising-dna.md) defines Tuba's promise, principles, and archetype in narrative form, this document renders that same strategy into the classic structured positioning formats used for onboarding, pitch decks, and quick strategic alignment.

## 1. The Formal Positioning Statement

> **For** Saudi property buyers, renters, sellers, agents, and developers **who** find real estate transactions confusing, high-stakes, and impersonal, **Tuba is** the real estate platform **that** guides you through the process with warmth, clarity, and verified trust — **unlike** competitors who lead with inventory scale or procedural trust-badges alone, **Tuba** treats every decision as a life moment worth genuine guidance, backed by the same regulatory rigor the category requires.

## 2. Positioning Pillars (condensed from advertising-dna.md)

1. **Guidance over inventory** — the request-first mechanic and the Sage/Guide archetype (advertising-dna.md §6) over "biggest database" claims
2. **Warmth with proof** — every emotional claim backed by a real, checkable trust signal (advertising-dna.md §8)
3. **Indigenously Saudi** — built from a Saudi brief from day one, not localized from an imported system (competitor-differentiation.md §1)
4. **Accessible-premium, not exclusive** — the default register serves the many, with a distinct luxury register reserved deliberately (advertising-dna.md §10, luxury-framework.md)

## 4. Positioning by Segment

| Segment | Positioning emphasis |
|---|---|
| Consumer (buy/rent) | Full positioning statement (§1) — guidance and warmth lead |
| Investor | "Confidence through clarity" — data-first framing (copywriting.md §5.7), same underlying guidance promise |
| Broker/Developer (B2B) | "A partner, not just a platform" — professional register (copywriting.md §5.2) applying the same guidance principle to a business relationship |
| Luxury | Restraint-and-specificity variant (luxury-framework.md) — the guidance promise expressed through discretion rather than broad warmth |

## 5. Positioning Guardrails

- Never reposition toward "biggest/most listings" — this cedes the one differentiated position Tuba credibly owns (competitor-differentiation.md §4)
- Never let the positioning statement's "unlike" clause become a public, competitor-naming claim — internal strategic language only (advertising-dna.md §5's no-disparagement rule)
- Any proposed repositioning must be checked against the full body of research in [competitor-analysis.md](../knowledge-base/competitor-analysis.md) and [project-analysis.md](../knowledge-base/project-analysis.md) before adoption — positioning changes are the highest-cost, hardest-to-reverse decisions this system governs

## 6. How to Use This Document

- **Onboarding a new team member or AI system:** read this before advertising-dna.md for the fastest structural grasp, then advertising-dna.md for the full narrative reasoning
- **Pitching a new campaign concept:** check it against §2's four pillars — a concept that doesn't clearly serve at least one pillar likely belongs in a different, non-Tuba-specific campaign
- **Evaluating a competitor's move:** re-plot the positioning map (§3) if a competitor's repositioning could close the gap Tuba currently occupies — flag this to competitor-differentiation.md and brand-memory/competitor-watch.md

## Best Practices
- Keep the formal positioning statement (§1) stable — it should rarely change wording, even as campaigns and tactics evolve beneath it
- Use the positioning map (§3) as a living diagnostic — re-assess competitor placement whenever competitor-analysis.md is refreshed

## Common Mistakes
- Treating positioning as a tagline-writing exercise rather than a strategic filter for what Tuba does and doesn't do
- Drifting the map's read of Tuba's own position based on aspiration rather than evidenced current reality — be honest about where Tuba actually sits today vs. where it's headed

## Future Expansion Notes
- Revisit the positioning map at each competitor-analysis.md refresh cycle; log any material shift (a competitor closing the warmth gap, a new entrant) to [brand-memory/competitor-watch.md](../brand-memory/competitor-watch.md) and escalate to a full review here if the shift is structural, not incremental
