# Market Language — Industry, Press & Analyst Register

> **Layer:** Brand Intelligence (static)
> **Purpose:** the formal register Tuba uses when speaking as an industry participant — press releases, market reports, investor/developer communication, regulatory correspondence. Distinct from [saudi-real-estate-language.md](saudi-real-estate-language.md) (how customers speak) and [real-estate-terminology.md](real-estate-terminology.md) (technical glossary).
> **Owner:** Marketing/Communications lead
> **Review frequency:** annually, or when engaging new institutional partners/press
> **Related documents:** [copywriting.md §5.2, §5.7](../advertising-system/copywriting.md), [content-system.md §4.7](../advertising-system/content-system.md), [competitor-analysis.md](../knowledge-base/competitor-analysis.md)

## 1. When to Use Market Register vs. Consumer Register

| Context | Register |
|---|---|
| Press release, market report, investor communication | Market Language (this document) |
| LinkedIn (B2B, developer/broker-facing) | Market Language, warmed per advertising-dna.md's voice adjectives |
| Consumer social, app copy, WhatsApp | saudi-real-estate-language.md |
| Legal/compliance disclosures | Most formal, plainest — see real-estate-terminology.md |

## 2. Preferred Market Vocabulary

مؤشرات السوق (market indicators), حجم المعاملات (transaction volume), معدل النمو السنوي (annual growth rate), القيمة السوقية (market value), التحول العقاري (real estate transformation — used carefully, echoes Vision 2030 macro-narrative without overclaiming), الشفافية التنظيمية (regulatory transparency).

## 3. Tone Calibration

Market/press language should be **confident and data-anchored, never promotional** — closer to how Bayut's "Saudi Market Insights" vertical or Aqar's "زبدة العقار" report series reads (competitor-analysis.md §6, §9) than to consumer ad copy. Avoid brand-voice devices that work in consumer content (direct address, emotional Hook framing) — market language leads with the finding, not the feeling.

**Example (market register):**
> "Transaction volume in the studied districts rose 14% year-over-year, driven primarily by [factor]. This aligns with broader national trends toward [context]."

**Not:** "Ever imagined how much your neighborhood has grown? You won't believe this number." (consumer register — wrong context)

## 4. Structural Conventions

- Lead with the finding/number, not a narrative hook (inverse of consumer Hook-first structure — copywriting.md §5.7)
- Always cite methodology/source, even briefly ("based on Tuba platform data, Q[x] [year]")
- Avoid superlatives entirely in this register — "significant growth" beats "explosive growth," "notable" beats "unprecedented," unless the underlying data genuinely and verifiably supports the stronger word

## 5. Regulatory/Compliance Correspondence

Most formal tier — full legal precision, no persuasive language at all. This register is out of scope for creative/marketing teams; route through Compliance (advertising-playbook.md §2 ownership matrix).

## 6. Press & Media Vocabulary

شراكة استراتيجية (strategic partnership), إطلاق (launch), منصة رائدة (leading platform — only when independently defensible, per advertising-dna.md §5's unverifiable-superlative rule), التزام تنظيمي (regulatory commitment).

## 7. Investor/Developer Communication Vocabulary

عائد على الاستثمار (ROI), جدوى المشروع (project feasibility), طلب مؤهل (qualified demand), شراكة تسويقية (marketing partnership) — see developer-personas.md and messaging-framework.md §10 for full audience application.

## Best Practices
- Every market-register document should be reviewable by someone with zero consumer-marketing context and still read as credible and precise
- When market data appears in consumer content (e.g., an Instagram stat card), it should be *simplified*, not just copy-pasted from a market report — see design-components.md §3.1 stat block spec

## Common Mistakes
- Bleeding consumer-register emotional language into press/investor communication — undermines credibility with a sophisticated audience
- Bleeding market-register dryness into consumer content — reads as cold and contradicts advertising-dna.md's warmth requirement
