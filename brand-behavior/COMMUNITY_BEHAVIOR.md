# Community Behavior

> **Part of:** [`../BRAND_BEHAVIOR_SYSTEM.md`](../BRAND_BEHAVIOR_SYSTEM.md)
> **Scope:** every place Tuba behaves in front of more than one person at once — reviews, referrals, neighborhood content, any social or shared feature. `narrative-system/CULTURAL_TRUTHS.md`'s Community entry establishes that Saudi community identity layers across family, neighborhood, region, and nation simultaneously; this document specifies how the product behaves in a way that respects that layering instead of flattening it into a generic "social feature."

---

## Referrals Are Never Incentivized Into Pressure

A referral program rewards genuine advocacy — it never frames not-referring as a missed opportunity or uses guilt/FOMO mechanics ("your friends are missing out!"). Per `advertising-dna.md §5`, the same ban on manufactured urgency applies to referral design as to any other growth mechanic. The desired feeling, from `advertising-dna.md §12`, is *"I want to tell someone about this"* — genuine, not incentive-engineered.

## Reviews Are Never Managed to Look Better Than They Are

Negative reviews are not suppressed, buried, or gamed with review-request timing designed to catch only satisfied moments. `advertising-dna.md §8.3`'s verification-enforcement standard extends here: a review system that isn't honestly representative is a trust violation, not a marketing optimization, and it directly repeats the exact failure the platform's own competitive analysis diagnosed in a competitor (`brand-intelligence/competitor-differentiation.md`'s review-sentiment gap finding).

## Neighborhood Content Respects the Real Texture of a Place

Per `narrative-system/CULTURAL_TRUTHS.md`'s Neighborhoods entry, a "good neighborhood" is described through duration and relationship — how long people have been there, what's genuinely walkable, which mosque, which bakery — never reduced to a generic amenities checklist. Any neighborhood-guide feature is written and sourced with this specificity as the bar, not filled with template-generated boilerplate ("close to shops and schools") that could describe anywhere.

## Community Features Respect the Layered, Non-Exclusive Nature of Belonging

Where the product supports any form of community or group interaction (a family sharing a search, a broker's local network, a developer's project updates reaching interested buyers), it never forces a person into a single primary community identity — a person can engage with Tuba as part of a family decision, a professional network, and an individual search, all without those layers needing to be reconciled into one account "type" the way `narrative-system/CULTURAL_TRUTHS.md`'s Community entry describes real Saudi social layering working.

## Sharing a Milestone Is Optional, Never Assumed

Per `SUCCESS_MOMENTS.md`'s household-respecting standard, any feature that would let a person share a milestone (new home, closed investment) with others is opt-in, low-key by default, and never auto-posts or auto-suggests broadcasting a private life event publicly.

## Hospitality as a Product Behavior, Not Just a Cultural Reference

`narrative-system/CULTURAL_TRUTHS.md`'s Hospitality entry — "a guest can tell within a minute whether hospitality is a habit or a performance" — has a direct product test: does the platform behave the same way toward a brand-new, first-time visitor as it does toward a long-standing customer, in terms of warmth and attentiveness? A product that saves its best behavior for existing customers and treats newcomers coldly has failed this standard, regardless of what its retention metrics say.

## What Community Behavior Must Never Do

- Never fabricate community proof (fake reviews, inflated activity counts, "X people are interested" claims that aren't real and current) — a direct extension of `BEHAVIOR_PRINCIPLES.md` §8.
- Never use community or social features to pressure a decision ("3 other families are considering this home").
- Never assume a single generic "Saudi community" voice across regionally and generationally distinct content — per `narrative-system/CULTURAL_TRUTHS.md`'s founding rule, specificity beats a flattened, single national voice every time.
