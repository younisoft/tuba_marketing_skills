# First Home Experience

> **Part of:** [`../BRAND_BEHAVIOR_SYSTEM.md`](../BRAND_BEHAVIOR_SYSTEM.md)
> **Persona grounding:** [`../brand-intelligence/buyer-personas.md`](../brand-intelligence/buyer-personas.md). A first home is the single highest-stakes, least-familiar decision most people using Tuba will ever make on the platform — this is the journey every other document in this folder is implicitly calibrated against, since `advertising-dna.md §2`'s entire brand promise, *"you will never have to figure this out alone,"* was written with exactly this person in mind.

---

## The Governing Shift: From Efficiency to Reassurance

Where `INVESTOR_EXPERIENCE.md` optimizes for analytical rigor and `BROKER_EXPERIENCE.md` for professional efficiency, a first-time buyer's journey is paced around one central need: reducing the fear of getting something this important wrong. Every behavioral decision in this journey is checked against `narrative-system/HUMAN_TRUTHS.md`'s Fear & Trust family (§31–40) before it's checked against conversion efficiency.

## Education Happens at the Point of Need, Never Front-Loaded

Per `EXPERIENCE_PRINCIPLES.md` §1, a first-time buyer is not handed a long guide to read before they're allowed to start. Explanation arrives exactly when a specific concept becomes relevant to their specific situation — what a deposit actually covers, right when they're facing that step, not in a general FAQ they were expected to have already read.

## Nothing Assumes Prior Transaction Experience, and Nothing Points That Out

The product behaves as if this is genuinely someone's first time, without ever making that visible as a judgment — no condescending "since this is your first time..." framing (`advertising-dna.md §4.2`'s ban on talking down to anyone). Extra context is simply present, offered as a normal part of the flow rather than flagged as remedial.

## The Wait Is the Hardest Part, and It's Designed For

Loan pre-approval, document verification, and offer acceptance all involve real waiting, and per `EXPERIENCE_PRINCIPLES.md` §3, this is where a first-time buyer's anxiety peaks hardest (`narrative-system/HUMAN_TRUTHS.md` §76: *"financial anxiety... shows up as sleeplessness at 2am with no obvious cause"*). Every meaningful wait in this journey has an honest status, a real timeline, and an easy way to ask a human what's happening — more so here than in any other persona journey in this system.

## Bad News Is Delivered With the Full Comfort Register, Never Delayed

A rejected application or an unavailable listing is communicated per `BEHAVIOR_PRINCIPLES.md` §7's speed standard, wrapped in `narrative-system/CONVERSATION_SYSTEM.md`'s "How Tuba Comforts" mode: naming the real situation plainly and respectfully, then offering the next concrete step — never vague reassurance that dismisses the disappointment instead of meeting it.

## The Household, Not Just the Account, Is Assumed

Per `EXPERIENCE_PRINCIPLES.md` §10 and `narrative-system/CULTURAL_TRUTHS.md`'s Family entry, a first home decision is rarely made by one person in isolation — a parent may need to be consulted, a spouse may need to review something separately. The flow accommodates a second decision-maker joining mid-process without penalty or friction, rather than assuming a single linear user completing the journey alone.

## Milestones Get the Full Tier 4–5 Treatment

A first home is, by definition, one of the largest `SUCCESS_MOMENTS.md` Tier 5 events the platform will ever mark for a given person — approval, offer acceptance, and especially keys handover are treated with the specificity and restraint that tier demands, never templated, because this is the single moment this entire behavior system was built to get right.

## What First Home Experience Must Never Do

- Never make budget or affordability conversations feel like a judgment of the person's financial standing (`narrative-system/HUMAN_TRUTHS.md` §72: *"nobody wants the cheapest option. They want to stop feeling watched for how much they can afford"*).
- Never rush a first-time buyer toward a decision faster than they're ready to make it, even where a faster decision would benefit conversion metrics.
- Never leave a first-time buyer without a clear, easy path to a human at any point in the journey — this persona's `HUMAN_ASSISTANCE.md` access should be the most visible and least effortful in the entire product.
