## Best Practices
- Regulatory observations should trigger immediate review, not wait for the monthly cadence — see [knowledge-log.md](knowledge-log.md) for logging the resulting decision
- Always cite a real, checkable source — never log a market observation based on rumor or unverified claims (the same evidentiary standard applied throughout this system)

## Common Mistakes
- Treating a single data point as a trend — distinguish a real shift from normal variance
- Failing to connect an observation to the specific document(s) it should update, leaving the insight stranded

## Future Expansion Notes
- Any regulatory observation should be cross-checked against [real-estate-terminology.md §1](../brand-intelligence/real-estate-terminology.md) immediately — that document explicitly commits to updating "immediately upon any REGA regulatory terminology change"
