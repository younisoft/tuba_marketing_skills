# Customer Feedback — Tuba

> **Layer:** Brand Memory (living)
> **Purpose:** the collection point for recurring real customer language — questions, complaints, praise, confusion, and suggestions — converted into concrete content and product opportunities rather than left as anecdotes.
> **Owner:** Marketing Strategy lead, fed continuously by Support/Customer-facing team
> **Update trigger:** continuously as feedback arrives; triaged weekly
> **Related documents:** [saudi-real-estate-language.md](../brand-intelligence/saudi-real-estate-language.md), [decision-triggers.md](../brand-intelligence/decision-triggers.md), [trust-framework.md §6](../brand-intelligence/trust-framework.md)

## Categories & Templates

### Questions (recurring, unanswered or under-answered by current content)

```markdown
**Question (as customers actually phrase it):** "[exact or near-exact wording]"
**Frequency:** [how often this comes up]
**Current content gap:** [does existing content answer this? where?]
**Opportunity:** [what content/product response this suggests]
```

### Complaints

```markdown
**Complaint:** [summary, neutral tone — no blame]
**Root cause (if known):** [link to trust-framework.md layer, or a product/process gap]
**Opportunity:** [trust-repair or content response — see trust-framework.md §6]
```

### Praise

```markdown
**Praise:** [what customers specifically valued]
**What this confirms:** [link to the brand-intelligence principle or campaign this validates]
```

### Confusion

```markdown
**Confusion point:** [what customers misunderstand]
**Likely cause:** [unclear terminology (real-estate-terminology.md), unclear process step, etc.]
**Opportunity:** [specific content fix]
```

### Suggestions

```markdown
**Suggestion:** [what customers are asking for]
**Feasibility note:** [marketing-addressable vs. product-team escalation]
```

## Best Practices
- Capture the customer's *exact* phrasing where possible — this is primary input for [saudi-real-estate-language.md](../brand-intelligence/saudi-real-estate-language.md)'s future updates, and paraphrasing loses the authentic register
- Triage weekly at minimum — feedback that sits unprocessed loses its connection to the specific campaign/context that generated it

## Common Mistakes
- Logging feedback without a proposed opportunity/next step — an observation without a forward action is incomplete (README.md §7)
- Treating a single comment as a pattern — wait for real recurrence before treating something as a content gap worth prioritizing

## Future Expansion Notes
- A question or confusion point that recurs 3+ times is a strong signal to create dedicated content addressing it, and to check whether [real-estate-terminology.md](../brand-intelligence/real-estate-terminology.md) or [copywriting.md §5.4](../advertising-system/copywriting.md)'s plain-language rules need reinforcement
