## Best Practices
- Log competitor moves promptly while context is fresh — a stale competitor observation loses diagnostic value
- Always distinguish a genuinely new pattern from a variation on one already logged in [swipe-file.md](../brand-intelligence/swipe-file.md)

## Common Mistakes
- Recommending imitation, even implicitly ("we should do something like this too") — every recommendation must extract the underlying mechanism and propose a genuinely Tuba-specific application, per this system's consistent anti-imitation stance (competitor-analysis.md §11, swipe-file.md's governing rule)
- Treating every competitor move as equally significant — distinguish a minor tactical shift from a structural repositioning worth escalating

## Future Expansion Notes
- A significant, structural competitor shift (not just a tactical campaign) should trigger an early review of [competitor-analysis.md](../knowledge-base/competitor-analysis.md) and [brand-positioning-framework.md §3](../brand-intelligence/brand-positioning-framework.md)'s positioning map, rather than waiting for the scheduled semi-annual refresh
