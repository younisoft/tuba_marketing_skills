# Conversation System

> **Part of:** [`../NARRATIVE_SYSTEM.md`](../NARRATIVE_SYSTEM.md)
> **Not copywriting.** [`../advertising-system/copywriting.md`](../advertising-system/copywriting.md) defines sentence-level rules — length, formulas, forbidden words. This document defines something upstream of all of that: the *relational modes* a person (or a brand acting like one) moves through in real conversation — how it asks, listens, celebrates, comforts, guides, thanks, congratulates, welcomes, and responds to criticism. Every piece of Tuba copy, in any format, is really just one of these ten modes, dressed for a specific channel.

---

## How Tuba Asks

Never a form-field question stripped of context. Tuba asks the way a good guide asks — one question at a time, explaining briefly why it matters, and always leaving room for "I'm not sure" as a valid answer. A question in this world is an invitation to think out loud, not an interrogation gathering data.

## How Tuba Listens

By responding to the *specific* thing someone said, never a generic templated reply. Listening, done well, is provable — it shows up as a reply that couldn't have been sent to anyone else's message. This is the conversational expression of the entire narrative system's specificity discipline (`WORLD_BUILDING.md`'s One-Sentence World Rule).

## How Tuba Celebrates

Quietly, and specifically — never with exclamation-point enthusiasm about a generic milestone. Tuba celebrates the way a close friend does: naming the actual thing that's worth celebrating, briefly, and then getting out of the way so the person gets to feel proud without an audience (`EMOTIONAL_SYSTEM.md`'s Pride entry — "never performed for an audience inside the story itself" applies here too).

## How Tuba Comforts

By naming the real fear plainly and respectfully, then offering the next concrete step — never with vague reassurance ("don't worry!") that dismisses the feeling instead of meeting it. Comfort in this world sounds like: *"That's a completely fair thing to be nervous about. Here's exactly what happens next."*

## How Tuba Guides

One step at a time, in the order a person can actually absorb them — never a wall of information delivered all at once because it was easier to write that way. Guidance here is paced to the listener, not to the guide's convenience.

## How Tuba Thanks

Briefly, specifically, and without lingering — Saudi hospitality instinct (`CULTURAL_TRUTHS.md`) reflected directly in conversational register: gratitude received simply, not effusively, and not turned into a marketing moment.

## How Tuba Congratulates

By making the moment about the person's decision, never about the platform's role in it. *"You did the hard part — you decided. We're glad we got to help,"* not *"Tuba made this happen for you."* (`../advertising-dna.md` Principle 1, at the conversational level.)

## How Tuba Welcomes

Warmly, specifically, and briefly — a welcome in this world names one real thing about the person or the moment, rather than defaulting to a generic greeting. It sounds like it was written by someone who was actually paying attention.

## How Tuba Responds to Criticism

Directly, without corporate deflection, and with a specific fix — never "we take this seriously" (already forbidden, `../brand-intelligence/trust-framework.md` §6's trust-repair protocol, which this conversational mode inherits without exception). Criticism is met with *"You're right, and here's what we're doing about it,"* not defensiveness dressed as professionalism.

## How Tuba Celebrates Milestones

By remembering them without being asked. A brand that has to be reminded a customer moved in a year ago hasn't been paying attention; a brand that remembers on its own has been listening the whole time. This mode is the conversational proof of `EMOTIONAL_SYSTEM.md`'s Gratitude and Nostalgia registers, expressed as an unprompted, well-timed message rather than a scheduled campaign trigger.

---

## The One Test for Every Mode

Before sending anything in any of these ten registers, ask: *could this exact message have been sent to anyone, about anything?* If yes, it hasn't actually listened yet — regardless of which of the ten modes it's operating in.
