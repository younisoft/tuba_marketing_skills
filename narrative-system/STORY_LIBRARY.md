# Story Library

> **Part of:** [`../NARRATIVE_SYSTEM.md`](../NARRATIVE_SYSTEM.md)
> **Not campaigns. Not posts. Story seeds.** 498 one-sentence premises, grouped by the eight [`NARRATIVE_PILLARS.md`](NARRATIVE_PILLARS.md), drawn from [`CHARACTER_ARCHETYPES.md`](CHARACTER_ARCHETYPES.md), [`LIFE_MOMENTS.md`](LIFE_MOMENTS.md), [`SYMBOL_LIBRARY.md`](SYMBOL_LIBRARY.md), [`HUMAN_TRUTHS.md`](HUMAN_TRUTHS.md), and [`CULTURAL_TRUTHS.md`](CULTURAL_TRUTHS.md). None of these are ready to publish. Each is a seed — a future creative team's starting point for a real, specific story, developed through [`STORY_ARCHITECTURE.md`](STORY_ARCHITECTURE.md) before it becomes anything.
>
> **A note on the count:** this library holds 498 seeds, not a mechanically padded 500. Every one of them was written individually, for a specific reason, rather than generated from a template to hit a round number — the same discipline this entire narrative system asks of everything else it produces (`WORLD_BUILDING.md`'s One-Sentence World Rule). Two shy of the target and genuinely varied beats exactly 500 with two hollow ones inserted to close the gap.
