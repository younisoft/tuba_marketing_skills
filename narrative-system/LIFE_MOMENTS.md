# Life Moments

> **Part of:** [`../NARRATIVE_SYSTEM.md`](../NARRATIVE_SYSTEM.md)
> **Not campaigns. Moments.** This is the raw material future stories are built from — the specific, small, universally recognizable instants that make a story feel true rather than staged. A campaign might use one of these moments as its entire visual center; a single story might move through three or four of them in sequence.

Each moment includes a one-line note on its emotional charge and which [`NARRATIVE_PILLARS.md`](NARRATIVE_PILLARS.md) it most naturally serves — not a rule, a starting point.

## How to Use This Catalog

A single future story rarely needs more than one or two of these moments — restraint is part of the craft (see [`STORY_ARCHITECTURE.md`](STORY_ARCHITECTURE.md)). The catalog exists so that no future creative team has to invent a moment from nothing; they choose one, then find the one unrepeatable detail that makes *this* instance of it different from every other time it's been told (`WORLD_BUILDING.md`'s One-Sentence World Rule).
